Welcome to SCG

Established in 2010, Stalworth Consulting Group (SCG) is a leading management and strategy consulting firm offering advisory capabilities that help organisations effect meaningful change through emerging technologies. We focus on technology project management, digital strategy and inclusive financial services.

Based in Uganda with associates located in Accra and Johannesburg, SCG has successfully completed over 132 engagements for public sector, social and private sector clients.

SCG is an independent contractor for Deloitte & Touche supporting Technology Advisory Services line of business and the clientele in sub-Saharan Africa.

What we do

We leverage on due diligence, digital finance, strategy and planning by providing high-level strategic, policy and investment advice to key institutions and corporations. We provide the local content across sectors and business functions to develop new technologies.

OUR SERVICES

SCG offers services in the following areas:

Consumer Insight & Assessments

We clearly define customer targets to enable more accurate matching of product features and benefits to the target audience and improve the ability to estimate market demand.

Due Diligence

We drive due diligence as an investigation of a business client's status or an act with a certain standard.

Policy & Regulation

Policy and regulatory planning serves as the principal to structure all matters of policy and organizational strategy. We formulate policies around new technologies for social impact.

Advisory Services & Business

We specialise in working with the small to medium-sized enterprises, mid-market businesses as technology advisors to drive transformative solutions

Strategy Impact Assessment

Assessment of social and economic impacts from the beginning to closure of a project to include the lessons learnt and strategy recommendations from the impact.

Strategy Evaluation Planning

We access strategic planning and evaluations to describe a program’s strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats and outline strategies and directions to implement.

Strategy Management Planning

A blueprint to strengthen program activities, address areas for improvement and move the program forward with new strategy recommendations.

Data Mining & Analytics

Information extraction or data analysis and data mining is the analytical step of the "knowledge discovery in databases" process. We drive data analytics and intelligence for decision support.

Project Management

We drive the discipline of initiating, planning, executing, controlling, and closing our work as a team to achieve specific goals for all phases of the project lifecycle.

The Team


PATRICK ADENGO


The team is led by Patrick Adengo, an accomplished technology and inclusive digital finance specialist and an expert on digital strategy, project management and operational risk. He helps organisations turn technology ideas by leveraging on due diligence, digital finance, strategy and planning for all phases of the project’s life cycle.

After completing his undergraduate and graduate studies at the University of Leeds, UK and the University of Nottingham, UK respectively, Patrick worked with Airtel, LM Ericsson International and Huawei Technologies scaling new products alongside global financial and data clearing houses. He is the technology advisor to the World Bank Group, the International Telecommunications Union (ITU) and Afrotech emerging technologies at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne.

Isaac Ndyamuhaki

As associate consultant at Stalworth Consulting Group, Isaac Ndyamuhaki handles pre-bidding consulting services, project proposal support, business development and draft pitching documents, delivers and implement the projects and support advisory engagements.

After completing his undergraduate studies degree at Makerere University, Kampala, Isaac worked on business analysis engagements through business incubations, innovations and entrepreneurship fellowships.

He is the Young AfricanLeaders Initiative (YALI) East Africa Fellow,2020 (Cohort 39), and associate Youth to Work Programme 2019.
